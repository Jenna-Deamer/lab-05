All About Me!

Growing Up

I was born in Newmarket Ontario, on October 12th, 2004. I spent about ten years living in Newmarket with my mother and two older siblings. I moved to Barrie at the beginning of 7th grade to be closer to other family members and post-secondary institutions.

Education & Achievements

I attended Innisdale secondary and graduated in June 2022. I mostly enjoyed my technology-related classes such as the introduction to programming using Python. During a handful of my computer courses, I was achieving above-average grades, and I spent a lot of my time during some of these classes helping other students.

Future Goals

I chose to attend post-secondary to better prepare for a career related to computer programming. I wanted to study computer programming because I enjoy working with computers and want to expand my knowledge. Currently, I am unsure what specific field I would like to go into. However, I hope to learn a lot about the different fields and find some that interest me.
